Questions founders ask before checkout

Will this run on normal STL exports from my slicer?

Yes. Motivora normalizes the STL's scale/origin, applies a neutral material, and centers it before rendering.

Can I keep rendering while away from my laptop?

The progress endpoint keeps the job alive, so you can close the preview and return later to download the finished clip.

Does Motivora support transparent background video?

Choose WebM VP9 for alpha-friendly exports, or MP4 for social channels that require H.264.

How do I charge customers?

Connect Stripe Checkout or Lemon Squeezy to sell Fast/Standard/Ultra credits, then point the success URL at this app.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free users get unlimited watermarked previews. Pro users can download watermark-free videos. All users can render unlimited videos.
